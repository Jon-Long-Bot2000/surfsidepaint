Meet Jon—tech founder turned painting contractor

After years in the tech industry, Jon traded his keyboard for a brush and never looked back. A local surfer and longtime fixture of the Southern California coast, he brings the same precision and problem-solving mindset to every paint job.

What started as weekend projects for friends turned into Surfside Paint—a one-man operation built on word of mouth, quality work, and genuine care for the community he calls home.

When he's not transforming spaces, you'll find him catching waves at dawn or hanging out at the local burn.
